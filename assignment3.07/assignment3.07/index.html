Graduate capstone Research Proposal

Research question: How does music help students in school with their mental health?


3 facts

Research has shown that it can lessen the impact of depression and anxiety

Music can provide them with a creative outlet to express their feelings and emotions, while allowing them to explore their identity and make sense of their experiences

Music therapy interventions can address a variety of mental health goals like promoting wellness and relaxation, managing stress, alleviating pain.
